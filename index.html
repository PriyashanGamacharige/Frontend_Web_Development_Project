About Our School

School of Information Communication Technology (SICT) intends to create graduates who are experts in their respective fields but also honest, responsible, enthusiastic, assertive, emotionally mature, confident, and value professionalism.

SICT will foster lifelong learning, critical thinking, and partner with like-minded communities spanning the globe in furthering human endeavors that serve all mankind using science and technology behind Buddhist teachings and practices.
