About SICT

Our History

The School of Information Communication Technology (SICT) was established in 2010 as part of Nāgānanda International Institute for Buddhist Studies. We've grown to become a leading institution for ICT education in Sri Lanka, combining modern technology with Buddhist values.

From humble beginnings with just two programs, we now offer a comprehensive range of ICT qualifications that are recognized both nationally and internationally.

Our Mission

To create graduates who are not only experts in their fields but also honest, responsible, and value professionalism. We foster lifelong learning and critical thinking while partnering with global communities through technology guided by Buddhist teachings.

We believe in education that transforms both the individual and society, creating technologists with strong ethical foundations.

Our Facilities

SICT boasts state-of-the-art computer labs, a comprehensive library with digital resources, modern lecture halls, and dedicated research spaces. Our campus provides a serene environment conducive to both learning and personal growth.

Students have access to high-performance computing facilities, specialized software, and high-speed internet throughout the campus.
